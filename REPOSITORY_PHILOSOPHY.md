# Repository Philosophy

This repository exists to preserve the institutional memory of Learn2Tape Publishing.

## Core Principles

**Every educational asset begins with curriculum.**

We do not create content and then organize it into learning. We define what clinicians need to learn, then express that curriculum through multiple media.

**Every publication has one canonical source.**

Blog articles are the canonical expression of Learn2Tape's educational voice. Carousels, emails, social posts, and future videos are derivatives—teaching methods for the same learning objective, not alternative versions competing for truth.

**GitHub is the single source of truth.**

Distribution platforms (WordPress, Brevo, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Canva, etc.) are execution channels, not archives. If something differs between GitHub and a platform, GitHub governs.

**Production is favored over redesign.**

The institution improves through published work and measured results, not through endless refinement of systems. Once a workflow proves itself through production, we use it, not redesign it.

**Improvements are earned through evidence.**

Changes to the publishing system, curriculum, or workflow only happen when:
- production exposes a genuine deficiency,
- analytics reveal a measurable problem, or
- a new product cannot be supported by the existing system.

Improvements are never made because an alternative seems theoretically better or more elegant.

## What This Means

Someone opening this repository in five years will find:
- The permanent educational curriculum that defines Learn2Tape
- The production workflow that transforms that curriculum into published assets
- The institutional history of decisions, learnings, and refinements
- A repeatable capability for publishing educational content at scale

They will not find endless governance layers, competing documentation systems, or abandoned experiments.

## The Compounding Engine

Every article published strengthens the institution.

Every carousel created reinforces the lesson.

Every newsletter sent builds familiarity with the voice.

Over time, this repository becomes not just a record of what was built, but evidence of what works.

That evidence becomes the foundation for everything that comes next.
