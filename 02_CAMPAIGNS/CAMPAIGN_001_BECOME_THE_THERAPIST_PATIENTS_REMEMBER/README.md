## Campaign Purpose

Introduce clinicians to the Learn2Tape curriculum through a structured 12-week educational journey.

This campaign expresses the core Learn2Tape teaching philosophy: **clinical reasoning comes before technique; education builds trust; trust drives enrollment.**

---

## Campaign Curriculum

Campaign 001 teaches the 12 core learning objectives of the Learn2Tape Curriculum across three phases:

### Phase I: See Differently (Weeks 1–4)

Readers challenge assumptions about taping and clinical reasoning.

- **LO1:** Clinical Reasoning as Foundation
- **LO2:** Assessment as the Foundation
- **LO3:** Confidence as a Clinical Skill
- **LO4:** Phase I Reflection (Capstone)

**Expected Outcome:** Readers understand that clinical reasoning, not technique, is the foundation.

### Phase II: Practice Differently (Weeks 5–8)

Readers integrate new approaches into established practice.

- **LO5:** The Taping Decision Tree
- **LO6:** Communication as Clinical Skill
- **LO7:** Measuring What Matters
- **LO8:** Phase II Reflection (Capstone)

**Expected Outcome:** Readers feel confident in new skills and see practice improvement.

### Phase III: Become Different (Weeks 9–12)

Readers internalize identity as clinical thinkers and educators.

- **LO9:** Teaching Others
- **LO10:** Professional Authority
- **LO11:** The Business of Clinical Excellence
- **LO12:** Phase III Reflection (Capstone)

**Expected Outcome:** Readers see themselves as clinical leaders and understand certification as identity, not credential.

## Campaign Audiences

### Primary Audience
Licensed massage therapists and athletic trainers actively practicing or pursuing certification.

### Maturity Levels
1. **New Graduate** — Recently certified, building skills
2. **Practitioner** — 2–10 years experience, seeking expertise
3. **Experienced Clinician** — 10+ years, mentoring others

### Campaign Positioning
"If you're a clinician who wants to think better—not just do more—this is for you."

## Campaign CTA Strategy

### Phase I: Build Awareness & Curiosity

- CTA: "Read the next article" (stay engaged)
- Secondary: "Join email" (ongoing education)
- Business: No enrollment CTA (too early)

### Phase II: Build Competence & Confidence

- CTA: "Implement and report" (action-driven)
- Secondary: "Explore the course" (soft enrollment signal)
- Business: Course page visits, not enrollments

### Phase III: Drive Enrollment

- CTA: "Understand why certification matters" (identity)
- Secondary: "Enroll in certification" (clear call-to-action)
- Business: Conversion (enrollment and revenue)

---

## Campaign Brevo Segments & Automations

### Segment 1: Awareness
- New subscribers from blog/organic
- Receive Phase I content
- Automation: 4-email Phase I sequence over 4 weeks

### Segment 2: Interest
- Opened 3+ Phase I emails
- Clicked on 2+ articles
- Automation: 4-email Phase II sequence over 4 weeks

### Segment 3: Consideration
- Engaged with Phase II (opened, clicked, read)
- Not yet enrolled
- Automation: 3-email Phase III + enrollment sequence over 4 weeks

### Segment 4: Active Learner
- Enrolled in any Learn2Tape product
- Receive ongoing community/bonus content
- Automation: Alumni engagement sequence
