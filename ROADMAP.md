## Principles Governing Evolution

### The System is Frozen Until Evidence Says Otherwise

No improvements, refinements, or redesigns without:
- Real production friction, OR
- Analytics revealing measurable problems, OR
- New products requiring changes

### Curriculum Comes Before Campaign

Every expansion of Learn2Tape publishing serves the 12 core learning objectives. New products do not create competing curricula; they express the same curriculum through different media.

### Production Before Perfection

The system improves through use and measurement, not theoretical refinement.

### One Source of Truth

GitHub remains the canonical repository. Changes happen here first; distribution platforms receive derivatives.
